Free-Textbooks University Consortium (FTUC)

Dear EE Faculty and Students:

Obtain Electrical Engineering Textbooks, directly from the authors, totally cost-free. We are offering three textbooks, in the form of downloadable free-of- charge PDFs, to any and all students and instructors interested in using these textbooks-- and associated websites-- in courses focused on circuits (2-semester sequence), signals and systems (2-semester sequence), and image processing.

Why free books? Very simple answer: Over the past 30 years, the consumer price index (CPI) increased by 240%. Over the same time period, textbook prices increased by 800%, more than a three-fold increase compared to the CPI! This venture represents a collective effort by the four authors listed below to remedy the textbook-price problem faced by students, albeit in only one narrow area within only one discipline, but itâ€™s a start.
Starting in 2008, we wrote two textbooks-- one on Electric Circuits and another on Signals and Systems-- and published them with National Technology and Science (NTS) Press, and managed to have them sold at a price under 50% of the market price of other books covering the same subject matter. The textbooks were adopted by a large number of universities and translated into other languages, which we interpret as a vote of confidence from our colleagues at the adopting universities. We recently managed to obtain permission from the publisher to produce new versions of the two textbooks and to either publish them with new publishers or to make them available for free. We decided on the latter option. We also completed a third book, on image processing, and we hope to attract other authors to join this venture in the coming months.
